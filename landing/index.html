Features

AI drafts. You review.

  • AI-assisted transcription

    Run segmentation and line HTR so the page arrives with proposed regions and readings. Review the AI work. Correct what needs a human eye. That is how you get more pages done.

  • Page editor

    Compare model output with ground truth on the same canvas. Edit segments or text in place. Keep layers separate per model or pass.

  • Local or cloud inference

    Use hosted inference by default, or the Inference Helper to run OCR and segmentation on your CPU with models cached on disk.

  • Projects and export

    Organize manuscripts into projects. Publish live pages. Export line crops and transcriptions for training pipelines.

About

Open research tooling

Nomicous is the annotation platform behind the NOMOS project: a hosted editor, open HTR models on Hugging Face, and an optional local inference helper. Weights sync from the registry so new models do not require reinstalling the helper.
